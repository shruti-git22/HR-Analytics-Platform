# TITLE : AtliQ HR Analytics Platform

## Overview :
Built HR analytics dashboard analyzing 3 months of employee attendance for 100+ staff using Power BI. Created dynamic data pipelines and 10+ DAX measures to track KPIs like presence rates, sick leave, and remote work. Developed multi-level filters, matrix visualizations and drill_through features.Delivered interactive reports enhancing workforce visibility and supporting targeted remote work interventions.Insights to be delivered to operations and product manager.

## Project Goals :
- To improve workforce visibility (attendance).
- Track employee performance.
- Schedule important meetings in best presence rate days.
- Conclude reasons for recurring absentee.
- Curate hybrid and remote work policies.

## Executive Summary : 

#### Presence - 91.8%
- Presentee fell from April(94.1%) to May(89.7%) by 4.6% and rose again by June(91.8%) by 2.3%.
- Presence dips at end of the week on Thursday(90.7%) and Friday(90.2%) than initial weekdays(92.7%) by 2.5%.

#### Work From Home - 10.0%
- WFH rate rose by April(9.1%) to May(11.2%) by 18.7% and dipped again in June(9.4%) by 16.1%.
- Increase in WFH on Thursday(11.5%) and Friday(13.0%) compared to initial weekdays(8.4%) by 68%.

#### Sick Leave - 1.1% 
- SL rate rose from April(0.4%) to May(1.7%) by 3.2% and dipped again in June(1.2%) by 0.3%.
- Increase in SL on Monday(1.6%) and Tuesday(1.2%) compared to other weekdays(0.8%) by 65.1%.

## Recommendations :
- Notice to Employees with below 90% attendance
- Implement Sandwich holidays scheme
- Launch wellness checks for employee health to reduce sick leave
- Offer commute allowance 
- Allow Work From Home for 2 days/week while promoting WFO through incentives
- Monitor SL and WFH if more than 3 days continuously.
